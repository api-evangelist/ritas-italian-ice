# Rita's Italian Ice (ritas-italian-ice)

Rita's Italian Ice is a Philadelphia-area founded franchise chain of frozen treat shops known for Italian Ice, Frozen Custard, Gelati, and Misto, operating approximately 550-590 locations across the United States under the tagline "Ice Custard Happiness." The brand is privately held under MTY Food Group (acquired 2021). Rita's maintains a consumer-facing loyalty mobile app and a franchise development site, but exposes no public developer API, OpenAPI specification, SDK, webhooks, status page, or developer portal at this time.
